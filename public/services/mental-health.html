Comprehensive Mental Health Care

Hopewell Hospital's Mental Health Services provide comprehensive psychological care for individuals of all ages. Our team of licensed psychologists, psychiatrists, and counselors offer evidence-based treatments in a supportive and confidential environment.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one therapy sessions addressing depression, anxiety, trauma, and other mental health conditions with personalized treatment plans.

Group Therapy

Therapeutic group sessions providing peer support and shared experiences for various mental health challenges.

Family Counseling

Family therapy sessions to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen family relationships.

Couples Therapy

Relationship counseling for couples facing communication issues, trust problems, or life transitions.

Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive psychiatric assessments, diagnosis, and medication management by board-certified psychiatrists.

Child Psychology

Specialized mental health services for children and adolescents including behavioral issues and developmental concerns.

Trauma Therapy

Evidence-based trauma treatment including EMDR and cognitive processing therapy for PTSD and trauma-related disorders.

Stress Management

Stress reduction techniques, mindfulness training, and coping strategies for managing daily stressors.

Addiction Counseling

Substance abuse treatment, addiction recovery support, and relapse prevention programs.
