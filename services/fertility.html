Comprehensive Fertility Care

Hopewell Hospital's Fertility Center offers state-of-the-art reproductive medicine services for couples and individuals experiencing fertility challenges. Our team of reproductive endocrinologists, embryologists, and fertility specialists provide personalized treatment plans to help you achieve your dream of parenthood.

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

Advanced IVF treatments with the latest laboratory technology and highest success rates for couples with various fertility challenges.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

Less invasive fertility treatment placing prepared sperm directly into the uterus for optimal conception timing.

Fertility Testing

Comprehensive fertility evaluation for both partners including hormone testing, imaging studies, and genetic screening.

Egg Freezing

Fertility preservation through oocyte cryopreservation for women who want to delay childbearing or preserve fertility.

Male Fertility Services

Comprehensive male fertility evaluation, semen analysis, and treatment for male factor infertility.

Genetic Testing

Preimplantation genetic testing and genetic counseling to reduce the risk of genetic disorders.

Donor Services

Comprehensive egg and sperm donor programs with extensive screening and matching services.

Fertility Counseling

Emotional support and counseling services throughout your fertility journey with licensed therapists.
